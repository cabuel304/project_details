Project Details

3D Model Creature

The inspiration from this model came from my love of science fiction creatures or other alien objects. Some of it came from Terraria and I tried to picture myself as a head model designer for a pixel game turned 3D. It is scary to see where my model creature would look like because I have a habit of not planning things and letting things happen once it feels right. This creature design has a more waterworld feel to it with tentacles and a claw which makes it more like a Subnautica creature.
