Project Details

3D Model Creature

The inspiration from this model came from my love of science fiction creatures or other alien objects. Some of it came from Terraria and I tried to picture myself as a head model designer for a pixel game turned 3D. It is scary to see where my model creature would look like because I have a habit of not planning things and letting things happen once it feels right. This creature design has a more waterworld feel to it with tentacles and a claw which makes it more like a Subnautica creature.

Movie Poster

When the pandemic hit, I was stuck on watching movies I have missed or have not seen. It wasn't until I heard that the Age of Resistance prequel to The Dark Crystal was cancelled on Netflix when I decided to watch the 1982 film. After watching, it was stuck in my for weeks. I loved the film for its story and character designs. The atmosphere blew me away.

The crystal has most of the main characters in their usual form. I wanted the crystal to showcase the personality of the movie and that I mean the beauty of puppeteer culture.

Short Story
