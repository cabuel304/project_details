About Me

I was born at Kapiolani Medical Center on April 30th, 2003. My education came from Ahuimanu Elementary, King Intermediate, James B. Castle High School, Windward Community College, and right now studying at University of Hawaii West Oahu. I am studying in General Creative Media and have done some personal projects in classes. I tend to have some passion in Creative Media but I do it for personal and fun projects. My passion as a designer is immense with thousands of ideas stored in my brain. Watching indie animation and analyzing designs made me want to become a designer.

I am very shy around people I do not know but I am willing to meet new people if I am confident enough. It ain’t easy to trust the person you meet just yet and that is okay.
